Where I'm From

My family comes from two different backgrounds. My dad's family has lived in geographically in New Mexico for hundreds of years, and my mom immigrated to the United States from the Philippines when she was a child. Because my parents come from two different backgrounds, I grew up experiencing a mix of the two cultures.

New Mexico

I grew up in Albuquerque, which is the largest city in New Mexico. Some things Albuquerque and New Mexico are known for are:

  • Albuquerque International Balloon Fiesta
  • Sandia Peak Tramway
  • Trinity Atomic Bomb Site
  • Breaking Bad
My favorite part about New Mexico is the food! New Mexico is famous for its chile, and my favorite New Mexican food is sopapillas.
